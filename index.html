SpiderLine (aka SpiderVine)

A human-powered, line-based access and movement system for regenerative work. Designed for forestry, camps, crossings, marine tending, and environments where care matters more than speed.

Overview

SpiderLine exists in two canonical forms:

  • 1-Cable SpiderLine — dynamic, skill-based, minimal material
  • 2-Cable SpiderLine — geometry-based stability, higher capacity

It integrates naturally with the Spoovel (field interface tool), the Garden Raft (staging and continuity), and an optional Spoovel Winch Module for hauling, retrieval, and emergency support.
