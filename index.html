MythGear: SpiderVine

Flexible quadricycle movement rig for land, vertical, or underwater use

Overview

The SpiderVine is a modular, open-source movement device that uses a hand-foot pedaling and cable-gear system to enable full-body locomotion along flexible or fixed cable routes. Ideal for mobility support, physical training, therapeutic movement, or safe underwater exploration.

Core Principles

  • Creative Commons build for grassroots, refugee, and off-grid settings
  • Full-body motion: engages arms, legs, and core
  • Modular system: reconfigurable for indoor, outdoor, or vertical/hanging use
  • Accessible: built from affordable, repurposed, or locally sourced parts

Possible Applications

  • Rehabilitation and strength training in small spaces
  • Youth mobility playframe or obstacle course
  • Underwater reef monitoring and restoration (minimal impact)
  • Vertical climbing frame for training, games, or access
  • Emergency extraction device for cable-assisted rescues
